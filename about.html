About Advanced Roofing Victoria

Victoria-based licensed roofing contractors. Family-run. Built on quality materials and honest workmanship statewide.

Our Story

Victoria's Roofing Specialists

Advanced Roofing Victoria was founded on a simple belief: every customer deserves honest advice, quality materials, and work that stands the test of time. We don't cut corners, and we don't over-promise.

Led by Mathew, our team has been delivering expert roofing across Victoria for over a decade. From straightforward gutter replacements to large-scale commercial re-roofs, we approach every project with the same level of care and attention to detail.

We work closely with homeowners, builders, developers, and architects — and we take pride in being easy to deal with from the first call through to final handover.
